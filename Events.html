Supra SAE

Held by the Society of Automotive Engineers India (SAEINDIA)

"SUPRA SAEINDIA Student Formula is a national engineering design competition held by the Society of Automotive Engineers India (SAEINDIA). Students gain and develop skills such as enginering, project management and team work."

Effi Cycle

The objective is to promote innovation and generate consciousness

EFFI CYCLE promote the objective of providing oppurtunity to the students to conceive, design and fabricate a three wheel configuration vehicle powered by human-electric hybrid power and capable of seating two passengers catering to the day to day mobility needs.

Baja India

Organized globally by the Society of Automotive Engineers, USA

The BAJA SAE Series® is an event for the undergraduate engineering students, organized globally by the Society of Automotive Engineers, USA. The BAJA SAE tasks the students to design, fabricate and validate a single seater four - wheeled off road vehicle.

IGC LPU

Global student challengep about knowledge of manufacturing and building up a go-kart.

International Go-Kart Championship is a global student challenge for Engineering students with a mission of encrypting students with technical as well as practical knowledge of manufacturing and building up a go-kart. The mission is accomplished by the ambition of these students to compete globally.

ESVC

Conducted by ISIEINDIA every year in March

ESVC is Asias Biggest Solar Challenge. It is conducted by ISIEINDIA every year in March Month. The Event (ESVC) is recognized solar car chain event by International Solar Car Federation in India.

AIDC

For those who love to design and innovate

Auto India Design Challenge is an event for those who love to design and innovate. It is an exciting opportunity for undergrads to present Their Designs of concept vehicle in front of Experienced judges from the automobile industry
