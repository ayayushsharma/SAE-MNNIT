ABOUT US

Our Vision

The vision of the club is to give young engineers an opportunity to showcase their talents, simultaneously providing a platform to learn and gain expertise in the fields of R&D, manufacturing associated with automobiles.

Our Mission

The club strives to promote the importance of practical experience, for the industry requires not only knowledgable persons, but also talented ones who can put that knowledge to use. This hands on approach is whatmakes the club stand out from the others.
