Faculty Corner
S A E M N N I T


Prof. K. N. Pandey Professor MED, MNNIT

Qualification:

Phd (UOA),M.Tech (IIT ROORKEE)

Area of Interest:

Fatigue and Fracture, Mechanical System Design, Design for X,Tribology.

It is very well said that- "Engineers are the world builders" but knowledge without the practical know-how is as good as a pencil without a lead. SAE India Collegiate Club of MNNIT is that platform which imparts the real time application of what is written in the books. The club is solely focused on making real technocrats in the field of automobile and has produced many from its foundation in the year 2014. Currently, the club comprise of more than 100 students from Mechanical and Production and Industrial Engineering and the family is ever increasing. Being a faculty advisor to such a club where the students work hard towards achieving their goals is a matter of great pride and responsibility. I wish them the best of luck for their future endeavours.

Dr. Jitendra N. Gangwar Asst. Professor MED, MNNIT

Qualification:

Ph.D(MNNIT ALLAHABAD). M.Tech(IIT KANPUR)

Area of Interest:

Internal combustion Engines, Combustion. Performance and Emission Characterization, Lubricating Oil Tribology, Alternative Fuels.

"Success of Technical Engineering College depends on how blackboard teaching can be converted to practical teaching". The SAE club of MNNIT Allahabad is one of the finest and unique clubs that do so. These students are involved in development of various automobiles such as racing cars, efficycle, electric bikes, hybrid cars etc. The students of this club works on design, analysis and fabrication of automobiles that are developed to take part in international events like SAE Supra, SAE Baja, SAE Efficycle and SAE Go Kart Championship. Till date the club has won several prizes in international championship being organized by Society of Automotive Engineers. In future this club will become the nucleus for the growth of practical technocrats.
